## Executive Summary

Engineering OS is a governance, cataloging, knowledge management and asset discovery platform designed to organize engineering, automation, AI and security assets.

The objective is to provide a standardized framework for documentation, reuse, governance and lifecycle management.

---

## Business Objective

Create a centralized engineering operating system capable of managing:

- Projects
- Workflows
- Agents
- Components
- Knowledge Articles
- Prompts
- Technologies
